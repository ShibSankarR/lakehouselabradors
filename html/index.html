Lake House Labradors

Hello my name is Katie Valdez and I'm the owner and operator of LakeHouse Labrador Retrievers LLC. My family has always had a love for dogs. When I was younger I would walk my neighbors dogs. Then I expanded into dog sitting. I had files on all the dogs, their birthdays, what treats they liked-I even baked my own treats for a while. I just had to be around them.

Puppies & Older Dogs

The retired dog program is a great option for people that do not want a puppy. Our retired dogs know basic, if not more, obedience commands. They are housebroken, current on shots and spayed or neutered. These dogs range in age from 4 to 7 years and they come with the lifetime support of the breeder.
